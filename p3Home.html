About Us

Our Cause

Firstly, you’re probably wondering what the heck a 2E student is- 2E stands for Twice-Exceptional which simply put means a student has both a high General Ability Index or IQ as well as some kind of learning disability, most commonly being ADHD and ASDs. The reason this group is so important to recognize is the same reason I had to explain it— we’ve only recently learned how large this population is and so far have done nothing do address their needs in our education system. One thing most people are familiar with is “Gifted Kid Burnout Syndrome” as dubbed by the internet, what you might be surprised to learn is this name might be a little misleading because studies have shown most of the burnout is happening with students who are much more than just gifted. A 2020 study stated that while there has been a trend of gifted students not reaching their supposed potential, most of these kids also have learning disabilities that are flying completely under the radar. [Bishop, 2020] Due to these reasons, and more that are explained in my About Us and this article I believe our municipal, state, and federal education departments need to collaborate on a third special program in schools, currently we have three classrooms in most schools— Special-Ed, Gifted Students, and “Normal”, what we actually need is a 4th classroom for children who fit the definition for both Special needs, and gifted.
